# Friendship Tracker App (KeenKeeper)

KeenKeeper is a personal relationship management tool designed to help you nurture and maintain your most important connections. In a busy world, it's easy to lose touch; KeenKeeper ensures your friends stay a priority.

## Technologies Used

- **React.js**: For building a dynamic and responsive user interface.
- **Tailwind CSS**: For modern, utility-first styling and responsive design.
- **Recharts**: For visualizing friendship analytics and interaction trends.
- **React Router**: For seamless navigation between dashboard, details, and timeline.
- **Motion**: For smooth, purposeful animations and transitions.
- **Lucide React**: For a consistent and beautiful icon set.
- **Sonner**: For elegant toast notifications.

## Key Features

1. **Intelligent Friendship Dashboard**: A high-level overview of your social health, featuring color-coded status indicators (On-track, Almost Due, Overdue) based on your personal contact goals.
2. **Interactive Interaction Timeline**: A searchable and filterable history of all your calls, texts, and meetups, allowing you to reflect on your shared history with friends.
3. **Friendship Analytics**: Visual data insights into your interaction patterns, helping you identify which types of communication you prefer and who your most active connections are.
4. **Quick Check-In System**: Log interactions with a single click directly from the friend details page, automatically updating their status and scheduling the next due date.

## Getting Started

The app uses local storage to persist your friend data and interaction history, making it a private and secure way to track your personal relationships.
